Contributions

I'm happy to take suggestions for new terms or changes to definitions. The best way is to submit a pull request to the source repository on GitHub.

Don't have time for that? Just tweet or DM me on Twitter (@peakscale). No problem.

You can also anonymously submit suggestions using this Google Form.
